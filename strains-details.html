Strain Overview

Fortune Cookies is a mixture of Girl Scout Cookies and OG Kush combined in a very powerful sativa hybrid. Fortune cookies is said to easily clear patients minds allowing them to enter a fully relaxed state of mind. The flavor of this strain is sweet and minty almost with a milky cream after taste. With its aroma closely identical to Girl Scout Cookies,but only presenting a slightly more pungent OG Kush scent, Fortune Cookies is often mistaken with Girl Scout Cookies throughout many dispensaries but dont let that trick you.This Strain is a great to smoke at night providing a mind clearing state that will eventually creep into a heavy body high. Girl Scout Cookies is known to have a decent CBD/THC Ratio and has been a favorite strain for pain relief among many patients. Fortune Cookies also carries this same ratio while presenting a much stronger body effect. The genetics, of both Girl Scout Cookies and Fortune Cookies, have been the subject of much speculation and rumor since their arrival on the market. Be attentive to detail when purchasing any Cookies strain as many false strains have been created to mimic their appearance and smell.
